[medical]
If a person is bleeding:
Apply firm pressure using clean cloth
Elevate injured area above heart level
Do not remove soaked cloth
Seek medical help immediately

[fire]
If there is fire:
Leave building immediately
Stay low to avoid smoke
Do not use elevators
Call emergency services

[accident]
If accident occurs:
Do not move injured person unnecessarily
Check breathing and consciousness
Call ambulance immediately

[natural_disaster]
During earthquake:
Drop, cover and hold
Stay away from windows
Move to open area after shaking stops
